Kids Mass Sheet - 5th Sunday After Easter

May 1st, 2013

I’ve had this idea for a while. Our local parish provides coloured worksheets for children to complete during Mass. They aren’t too bad, but I’ve been thinking we could do something a little better. Working with the Latin Mass has the advantage of not having to cater for three years worth of Sundays (A, B and C). So, here’s the challenge. Someone at Brandt Lab to make a kids sheet each week for the next year. It should include a colouring picture and some words to trace. I’m using Christopher Jarman’s italic font here.

The first installment: Easter5
